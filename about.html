About Us

Our Story

J&L Paving, Excavating & Trucking is a family‑owned and operated business proudly serving Charleston, West Virginia and the surrounding counties. We’ve built our reputation on honest work, fair prices and a deep commitment to our community.

With decades of combined experience in asphalt paving, site excavation and material hauling, our team knows what it takes to get the job done right. We take pride in every driveway we pave, every site we grade and every load we deliver.

We are fully licensed and insured, and we treat your project as if it were our own. From residential driveways to commercial lots, no job is too big or too small.

Why Choose Us

  • Experience: Years of hands‑on experience in paving, excavation and hauling.
  • Quality Materials: We use high‑grade asphalt and aggregates to ensure a long‑lasting result.
  • Professional Crew: Our team is trained, courteous and dedicated to customer satisfaction.
  • Local & Family Owned: We live and work in the Charleston area – we’re your neighbors.
